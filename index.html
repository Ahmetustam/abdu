About Us

What is bot Bot?

True is a Discord Bot, built the the latest technologies which will help you and your staff to manage, secure and supervise your Discord Server in the best way.

How can I invite bot in my Discord Server?

If you want to invite True in your server, you can at the top of the page and click one of the button saying "Invite bot Now".

Can I customize bot?

Of course. If you want, you can change the prefix, so that your guild will have a custom prefix (the default one is t!).

You can also change the bot's channels, which are #✅verify and #👀logs.

Finally, you can change the bot's roles, which are ✔Verified and ❌mute.
